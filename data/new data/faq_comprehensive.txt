GENERAL INFO:
01). What is the website about?
It’s the official portal of the Telangana state agency responsible for organising and overseeing sports and athletic programs across Telangana.

02). Can you tell me about Sports Authority of Telangana?
Sports Authority of Telangana (SATG) is the state government body responsible for promoting, developing and managing sports infrastructure and athletic talent in Telangana.

03). What are these Sports or Games conducted for?
The sports and games conducted by the SATG are intended to identify talent, provide professional training and prepare athletes for state, national and international competitions.

04). On what time basis the Sports and Games are conducted? (monthly or annually or for every 2 years, etc.)
The sports and games by the SATG are conducted throughout the year, with daily training and seasonal camps.

05). What are the past Achievements of Sports Authority of Telangana?
Please refer to the Achievements section on the official website.

06). Contact Information.
Refer to the Contact Us section on the website. Helpline: 040-2323 2323.

07). What are the available online services?
Available online services include “Play & Play”, “Summer Camp”, “CM's Cup”, and “TGSS Admissions”.

08). What are the Key Initiatives by SATG?
Key initiatives include statewide talent identification, year-round coaching camps, CM’s Cup competitions, infrastructure development and inclusive sports promotion.

09). Past Award Winners.
Please visit the Awards section on the home page.

10). Location of the Headquarters?
The headquarters is located at L.B. Stadium, Hyderabad.

PAY & PLAY:
01). How to register for Training camps or Regular Trainees?
To register for SATG as a regular trainee, visit the ‘Regular Trainee's Registration’ page, fill the online registration form and complete the payment.

02). Is there any fee to register as a Regular Trainee?
Yes, the SATG charges a registration and/or monthly fee for regular trainees, depending on the sport and facility.

03). What are the available disciplines?
The SATG offers disciplines such as athletics, badminton, boxing, cricket, football, kabaddi, kho-kho, swimming, wrestling, tennis, table tennis, basketball, volleyball, hockey, yoga, gymnastics, cycling, shooting, chess, judo, karate and martial arts.

04). What is the fee structure?
The fee structure under the SATG varies by discipline and facility. For example, different venues like L.B. Stadium, Saroornagar, etc., may have specific rates.

05). What are the available online services?
SATG “PAY & PLAY” online services include Athlete/Trainee registration, Tennis-Court & venue booking, online payment (Admission or Monthly fee) and download of receipts/ ID-cards.

06). What are the available payment methods?
You can pay on the SATG portal via online payment gateway using debit/credit card, net-banking or digital wallet.

07). What are the timings for Training camp?
Typically, training camp timings run in two daily sessions: morning and evening.
